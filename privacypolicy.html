PRIVACY POLICY
EFFECTIVE JULY 15, 2023
We appreciate you visiting The Estrakt. Your privacy and the security of your personal information are important to us. The information you give us when accessing or using our Blog is collected, used, disclosed, and protected in accordance with the terms of this Privacy Policy. You accept the conditions and guidelines outlined in this privacy statement by visiting or using our blog.
Collection of Information
We may gather some information about you when you visit or use our blog, including:
Personal Information: When you sign up for our newsletter, leave comments, or contact us via the available channels, we may gather personal information from you, such as your name, email address, and any other details you freely supply to us. We only use your information to react to your enquiries and to deliver the services you have requested.
Log Files: Similar to many websites, when you visit our Blog, we automatically gather certain non-personal information. Your IP address, browser type, operating system, date/time stamps, and clickstream data are among the details included in this list. We use this data to spot patterns, manage the blog, follow users across the site, and compile broad demographic data. This data is not linked to any personally identifiable information.
Use of Information
We may use the collected information for the following purposes:
Personalization: We may customise your experience on our blog by presenting material or making recommendations depending on your interests and choices based on the information you submit.
Communication: We may use your contact information to answer your questions, provide you the material you've asked, and update you on our blog or other pertinent information. By following the instructions in the email or getting in touch with us directly, you can choose not to receive these communications at any time.
Analytics and Enhancements: We may use the information collected to measure the effectiveness of our content, analyse user behaviour and preferences, and make adjustments to optimise your experience on our blog
Disclosure of Information
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:
Service Providers: Your personal information could be disclosed to dependable third-party service providers that help us run our Blog, carry out our business operations, or offer you services. These service providers are required to maintain the privacy of your information and may only use it as directed by us.
Legal Requirements: If obliged to do so by law or in the good faith belief that doing so is necessary to comply with legal requirements, protect and defend our rights or property, or look into possible violations of the terms and conditions of our Blog, we may disclose your personal information.
Uses of AI Technology
We want to let you know that some of the posts on the Tech, Entertainment, and Gaming Blog have been refined using artificial intelligence (AI) technology. While our team of human writers and editors produce the majority of our material, AI algorithms are occasionally employed to analyse data, trends, and patterns in order to help our authors produce more thorough and insightful content. It's vital to remember that before publishing, our human authors always evaluate, revise, and approve the final text. We can ensure you that this Privacy Policy will be followed with regard to any data that is gathered, processed, or utilised in connection with AI technology.
Data Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
Third-Party Links
Our Blog may contain links to third-party websites or services.
